Welcome to the Rise of Cyrus project!

This is a passionate solo indie game development effort inspired by the remarkable legacy of Cyrus the Great and the rich history of the Persian Empire. The goal is to create an immersive and engaging gaming experience that brings this fascinating era to life.

Whether you’re interested in voice acting, 3D modeling, game design, or any other creative contribution, your skills and passion are very welcome. Collaboration and community are essential to this project’s success, and every contribution helps bring the vision closer to reality.

For more details about the project, how to get involved, and the latest updates, please visit the respective README file in your preferred language.

Thank you for stopping by — your interest and support mean a lot!
